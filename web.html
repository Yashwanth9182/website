Career Paths

  • Bookseller: Assist customers in finding books, provide recommendations, and manage sales transactions.
  • Store Manager: Oversee daily operations, manage staff, and ensure sales targets are met.
  • Inventory Specialist: Organize and manage book stock, ensuring timely restocking of popular titles.
